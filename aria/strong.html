strong – strong importance # T

The strong element represents a span of text with strong importance.

Content model #

strong = (normal-character-data & common.elem.phrasing*) & strong.attrs

Attribute model #

strong.attrs = common.attrs & common.attrs.aria?

Element-specific attributes #

Tag omission #

A strong element must have both a start tag and an end tag.
